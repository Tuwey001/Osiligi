About OGEN

Founded in 2017, we're dedicated to empowering Maasai girls through education and advocacy

Since 2017

Osiligi Girls Empowerment Network

Founded in May 2017 and registered in November 2018, OGEN is a community-based organization dedicated to empowering girl children and inspiring them towards great achievements in life. We operate from Narok town, Narok County in South Rift Kenya.

Addressing FGM

Combating Female Genital Mutilation through education and community dialogue

Ending Child Marriage

Fighting forced early marriages and ensuring girls complete their education

Quality Education

Providing sponsorship and support for girls to access quality schooling

Our Vision
