Flagship Program

Educational Support Program

Our flagship program helps girls stay in school through comprehensive mentorship, advocacy, and provision of essential learning materials. We work with girls, families, and schools to remove barriers to education and create pathways to success.

Mentorship and career guidance
Provision of learning materials
School fee support programs
Teacher collaboration and advocacy
After-school tutoring and support
Parental engagement workshops
15+ Girls in School
100% School Retention

Critical Support

Rescue, Co-Parenting & Reconciliation

We provide immediate rescue for girls at risk and offer comprehensive support including safe shelter, counseling, and family reconciliation services. Our holistic approach promotes healing and successful reintegration into supportive environments.

Emergency rescue services
Safe shelter and basic needs
Psychological counseling
Family mediation and reconciliation
Legal support and advocacy
Long-term follow-up care
50+ Girls Rescued
85% Successful Reintegration
24/7 Support Available

Community Empowerment

Cultural sensitivity training
