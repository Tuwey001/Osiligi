Our Journey

Our Story

OGEN was founded to respond to the multifaceted challenges facing the Maasai girl child. In the heart of Narok County, Kenya, our organization emerged as a beacon of hope, dedicated to transforming lives through education, advocacy, and empowerment.

What started as a small initiative has grown into a comprehensive network of dedicated individuals, volunteers, and partners. Today, OGEN continues to work relentlessly to ensure that every girl has access to quality education, safety, and the opportunity to reach her full potential.

Our journey reflects the resilience and determination of the communities we serve, and our commitment to building a brighter future for generations to come.

Vision & Mission

Guiding our work towards a better future for girls

Our Vision

A society where girls are safe, educated, and free from harmful cultural practices. A community where every girl child has the dignity, rights, and opportunities to become a leader and change-maker in her own right.

Safe from harmful practices
Access to quality education

Our Mission

To build strong partnerships and sustainable networks that uphold the dignity, education, and rights of girls. We achieve this through holistic interventions in education, rescue, advocacy, and community empowerment.
